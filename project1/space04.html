It could've been space debris, our own creations sinking back to Earth. It could've also been ancient rock that went to our planet by pure chance.
Either way, it was something special to witness and something I haven't witnessed again even 2 years later.

Is there anything to take away from this story?

To enjoy the moment?
To enjoy even what seems like the little things>
To always have a camera be prepared for anything?

Truth is there is no moral, not everything needs one! But at least someone else has heard my story!

Now if you don't mind me, I'm heading back to the roof.
