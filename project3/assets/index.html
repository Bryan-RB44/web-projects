[[Out of Bounds]]

The one spot to relax and post
at after your game's over

Welcome to Out of Bounds! The site to come to after playing your favorite games and talk about them! You ever get frustrated with game design? Completely relieved after a good run? Invested in the deep lore of the story? Absolutely crazy after getting a rare item? Some weird mix of any of the above!?

The general rule here is that you post as if you yourself are coming DIRECTLY from the game you played!
(ex. Just played The Sims? Congrats, you're now the maniac who took away the ladders from the swimming pool!)

Whether you're the protagonist, a bodyless entity in a simulator game, or literally even a puzzle block, it's all fair game! Anyone from NPC's to enemies are also allowed to post, so feel free to post from their perspectives!

All genres are allowed and any game consoles are allowed, just have some fun with it!
